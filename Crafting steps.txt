Rule 10 - Cold Phys / BV / Reap Scepter.

Step #1: Get a 3 mod explody synth base.

Step #2: Imprint + roll implicits a billion times until you hit desired prefixes (frostbite + %inc)

Step #3: Alt roll for +1 all and imprint.

Step #4: Regal and clean.

Step #5: Prefix cannot be changed + Aisling (or veiled chaos). If you use a veiled chaos and it bricks with lots of caster mods, you can restore from imprint. Both methods Aisling / Vchaos works. All depends on the price of Aisling.

Step #6: Repeat #5 until a veiled prefix and block % phys before unveiling. Repeat until %spell + non chaos as chaos.

Step #7: Multimod + pref cannot be changed + cannot roll attack + aug phys.

Step #8: Pref cannot be changed + aug crit spam until T1 crit to spells + T1 crit multimod.

Step #8b: If you shit the bed with augs you can always pref cannt be changed and scour and repeat #8.

Cost:

Base rolling alone was more than a mirror. Not sure if lucky or not. The odds table is pretty outdated at this point so can't really tell how much it should cost on average. The craft itself went swimmingly this time around. The aug crits have a decent chance to hit T1/T1. The only problem is that on the second Aug I remove a previously spawned T1 many times (50/50 chance). So things ended up being somewhat painful for a while but it's fine considering the outcome.

Total craft cost 2 mirrors. Although I doubt it could be replicated for that amount or less due to the high RNG on the implicit rerolling part. Along the way I hit a couple other synth bases that I still have and will be crafting later on.

Conclusion:

Synth base rolling is fun if you hit something, if you don't, it is a currency sink like no other. I don't recommend it at all. So be wary before you try it, you might get burned pretty bad.
